CRYSTALS COLLECTOR

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four CRYSTALS below. By clicking each CRYSTAL, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score is more than the random number.

The value of each CRYSTAL is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, each CRYSTAL will have different values than in preceding games.
